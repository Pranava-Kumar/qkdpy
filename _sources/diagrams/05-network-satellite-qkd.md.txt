## Satellite Pass Simulation

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant User as User
    participant SAT_API as SatelliteQKD
    participant ORBIT as OrbitCalc
    participant ATMOS as Atmosphere
    participant CH as QuantumChannel

    User->>SAT_API: simulate_pass(duration=300s, atmosphere)
    SAT_API->>ORBIT: compute_orbit(altitude, lat, lon)

    loop Every time step (0.1s)
        ORBIT->>SAT_API: elevation_angle, slant_range, velocity

        SAT_API->>ATMOS: atmospheric_transmittance(elevation)
        ATMOS-->>SAT_API: transmittance, turbulence, scattering

        SAT_API->>CH: compute_channel_loss(slant_range, transmittance)
        CH-->>SAT_API: loss_probability, quantum_bit_rate

        SAT_API->>SAT_API: accumulate_key_bits()
    end

    SAT_API-->>User: {total_key_bits, key_rate,<br/>elevation_profile, channel_stats}
```
